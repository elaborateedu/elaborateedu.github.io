How it works

The hiring process.

01
~5 minutes
Apply
Fill out the application for the role you want. Tell us who you are, why Elaborate, and what you bring. No resume required — just be honest and specific.
We read every single application. Generic answers are easy to spot. The best applications are the specific ones.
02
~1 week
We Review
The owner and a senior moderator review your application together. We look for genuine interest, clear communication, and alignment with the role. Everyone hears back — no ghosting.
We are not looking for perfection. We are looking for people who are honest about what they know, curious about what they do not, and genuinely excited about what Elaborate is building.
03
~1 week
Trial Phase
Accepted applicants enter a supervised trial period. Work alongside the team on real tasks, get direct feedback, and get a feel for how we operate. You also get to evaluate us.
This is a two-way evaluation. We want to make sure we are worth your time as much as you are worth ours.
04
Ongoing
Full Member
You get your role, access to Staff Connect and the admin panel, and you start shipping. Moderator, Sr. Moderator, and beyond are earned through impact over time — not applied for.
A note on the Owner rank: this is not a position that is hired for. It is the founder role and is not open. All other senior roles are earned through internal promotion.
