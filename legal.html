Privacy Policy — Effective January 2025

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains what information Elaborate collects, how we use it, and how we protect it. We take your privacy seriously — especially because many of our users are students.

The short version: We collect only what we need to make the service work. We never sell your data. We never show you ads.

2. Information We Collect

When you create an account and use Elaborate, we collect:

  • Account information: Your first name, last name, and email address

We do not collect browsing history, track which apps you click, or monitor your activity within the apps you launch from your hub.

We use the information we collect solely to:

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore and Authentication), which provides industry-standard encryption at rest and in transit. Firebase is operated by Google LLC and subject to Google's security standards.

Data is stored on servers located in the United States.
