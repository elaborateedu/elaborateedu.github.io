FAQ
Common questions.
How do I get a district set up? ▼
Submit a support ticket on our Support page and select "Request a District" as the category. Include your school or district name, your role, and roughly how many teachers and students you have. We'll set everything up and email you your district admin access within a few business days.
Do teachers need to change their role to join a district? ▼
No. Teachers keep their existing teacher account. They simply enter the district join code in Settings → District, and they're connected. Their classrooms and students are unaffected.
How do students get district apps? ▼
Automatically. When a student joins a teacher's classroom and that teacher is part of your district, the student inherits all district-pinned apps and branding instantly. No join code or extra steps required from students.
Can I control which apps teachers and students can remove? ▼
Yes. In your District Dashboard under "District Apps," you can lock any pinned app. Locked apps appear in everyone's hub but cannot be removed by teachers or students — perfect for required tools.
Can multiple people be district admins? ▼
Currently one admin account is set per district during setup. If you need to transfer ownership or add a co-admin, contact support and we can update it manually. Multi-admin support is on our roadmap.
What happens if I cancel my subscription? ▼
Your district will move to the free trial tier. All teachers and students keep their accounts and data. District-pinned apps and custom branding will be paused but not deleted — if you resubscribe, everything is restored exactly as it was.
Is student data safe? ▼
Yes. Elaborate stores only the data needed to run the hub — names, emails, and app preferences. We don't sell data to advertisers, we don't run ads, and district data is kept completely separate from other districts. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
Can I try it before committing to a paid plan? ▼
Absolutely. All new districts start on a free trial with full access to the dashboard, app management, and announcements. No credit card required. When you're ready to unlock full branding and priority support, you can upgrade anytime.
