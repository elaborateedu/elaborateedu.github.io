Admin

The backbone of the platform. Admins keep the community safe — reviewing tickets, supporting users, and making sure Elaborate stays a great place to learn.

  • Reply to and manage support tickets
  • Issue warnings and suspend accounts
  • View user profiles and history
  • Use canned responses and internal notes

  • Contribute code and features
  • Review and report bugs
  • Access to dev discussion channels
  • Shape the technical roadmap
  • No moderation authority
  • Codebase access via invite only

What We're Looking For

Requirements & Expectations

  • 01You must be at least 14 years old and have an active Elaborate account.
  • 02Genuine care for students and teachers — this is an educational platform and our users come first.
  • 03Ability to communicate clearly and professionally in written English.
  • 04Willingness to contribute when you can — we have no minimum hour requirement and understand you have a life outside this.
  • 05Willingness to follow platform guidelines and make fair, consistent decisions.
  • 06Reliable access to the internet and a device that can run a modern browser.

Nice to Have

  • →Experience with customer support, moderation, or community management.
  • →Background in education, tutoring, or working with students.
  • →Basic web or software knowledge (especially for developer applicants).
  • →Previous experience on an admin team for any platform or community.
  • →Ideas for features or improvements — we love builders.

This is a volunteer position. There is no monetary compensation. What you do get is real experience, a great team, and the satisfaction of building something students genuinely use every day.
