Support Center

Having trouble? Submit a ticket and we'll look into it.

Submit a Ticket

Fill out the form below and we'll get back to you as soon as we can. Note: For smaller issues we will reply within a week. For larger issues it may take us 2 weeks to a month.

📋 What to include in your request
  • Your district or school name
  • Your role (principal, IT admin, etc.)
  • Estimated number of teachers and students
  • Any specific apps or tools you'd like pinned district-wide
  • Preferred subscription plan (Trial / Monthly / Annual)
🛠 Tips for developer tickets
  • For app submissions — fill in the submission form on the Developers page instead, it is faster
  • For listing updates — include your app name, current URL, and what needs to change
  • For integration questions — include a link to your app and describe what you are trying to build
  • For partnership inquiries — include your company name, what you do, and what kind of partnership you have in mind

Common Questions

How do I add a custom app? ▼
Go to your hub and click the "Add App" card at the end of the grid. You can give it a name, URL, icon, and color.
I forgot my password. ▼
On the login screen, use the "Forgot password" link to reset it via email. If you signed up with Google, use Google Sign-In instead.
Why was my account disabled? ▼
Accounts can be disabled for violating our usage policy or at the request of a school administrator. Submit a ticket with the "Suspension appeal" category to contest it.
Is Elaborate really free? ▼
Yes — completely free. No ads, no premium tiers, no hidden fees.
How do I change my theme? ▼
Click your avatar in the top right → Settings → Theme. Pick from Default, Dark, Ocean, and Forest.
How long does support take to reply? ▼
For smaller issues, up to a week. For larger issues like suspension appeals, it can take 2 weeks to a month.
