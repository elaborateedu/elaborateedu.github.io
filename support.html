Common Questions

How do I add a custom app? ▼
Go to your hub and click the "Add App" card at the end of the grid. You can give it a name, URL, icon, and color.
I forgot my password. ▼
On the login screen, use the "Forgot password" link to reset it via email. If you signed up with Google, use Google Sign-In instead.
Why was my account disabled? ▼
Accounts can be disabled for violating our usage policy or at the request of a school administrator. Submit a ticket with the "Suspension appeal" category to contest it.
Is Elaborate really free? ▼
Yes — completely free. No ads, no premium tiers, no hidden fees. It's a student project built for students.
How do I change my theme? ▼
Click your avatar in the top right → Settings → Theme. You can pick from Default, Dark, Ocean, and Forest.
