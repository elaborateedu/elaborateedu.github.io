Open a Support Ticket
Describe your issue and we will get back to you. For smaller issues expect a reply within a week; larger issues may take up to a month.
01 — About You
02 — Your Issue
🐛 Reporting a bug
  • Describe exactly what you did, what you expected, and what happened instead
  • Include your browser and device (e.g. Chrome on Windows 11)
  • Paste any error messages you see in the console or on screen
📋 Suspension appeals
  • Include the email address tied to your account
  • Explain why you believe the suspension was issued in error
  • Appeals are reviewed within 2 weeks
🏛 District account requests
  • Your district or school name and location
  • Your role (principal, IT admin, superintendent, etc.)
  • Estimated number of teachers and students
  • Preferred subscription plan (Trial / Monthly / Annual)
🛠 Developer tickets
  • For new app submissions — use the submission form instead, it is faster
  • For listing updates — include your app name, current URL, and what needs changing
  • For API or integration questions — include your app URL and what you are building
🤝 Partnership inquiries
  • Your company or organization name and what you do
  • The kind of partnership you have in mind
  • A contact email and best time to reach you

Common Questions
How do I add a custom app?▼
Go to your hub and click the Add App card at the end of the grid. Give it a name, URL, icon, and color. It will appear in your hub immediately.
I forgot my password.▼
On the login screen click "Forgot password" to reset via email. If you signed up with Google, use Google Sign-In instead — there is no separate password.
Why was my account disabled?▼
Accounts can be disabled for violating the usage policy or at the request of a school administrator. Submit a ticket with the Suspension Appeal category to contest it.
Is Elaborate free?▼
Yes, completely free for students and teachers. No ads, no premium tiers, no hidden fees. District plans are available for schools wanting additional features.
How do I change my theme?▼
Click your avatar in the hub top-right, go to Settings, then Theme. Choose from Default, Dark, Ocean, and Forest.
How do I set up my class schedule?▼
Click the Schedule button in the hub sidebar. You can add periods manually or import from Follett Aspen using an .ics file. Block day schedules (A/B/C) are also supported.
How do I join a classroom?▼
Ask your teacher for the classroom code. In your hub, click Join Classroom in the sidebar and enter the code. You will see teacher-pinned apps and announcements immediately.
Can I submit an app to the library?▼
Yes. Visit the Developers page and fill out the app submission form. Apps are reviewed by the team before being added to the public library.
How long does support take to reply?▼
For most issues: within a week. For suspension appeals and larger requests: up to a month. All tickets are read by a real team member.
How do I delete my account?▼
Submit a Privacy concern ticket with your account email and request deletion. We will process it within 30 days in accordance with our privacy policy.
