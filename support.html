Common Questions
How do I add a custom app?▼
Go to your hub and click the Add App card at the end of the grid.
I forgot my password.▼
Click Forgot password on the login screen. If you used Google Sign-In, click Continue with Google.
Why was my account disabled?▼
Accounts can be disabled for policy violations or by a school admin. Use the Suspension Appeal category to contest it.
Is Elaborate free?▼
Yes, completely free for students and teachers. No ads, no premium tiers.
How long does support take to reply?▼
Most issues: within a week. Suspension appeals and larger requests: up to a month.
