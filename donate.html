Common questions
Will students ever have to pay? ▼
Never. Students and individual teachers always use Elaborate completely free. The subscription is only for school districts wanting admin-level control.
How does the district subscription work? ▼
When you subscribe, you get a District Admin account and a join code. Teachers enter the code in their Settings to join the district. You then manage apps and branding for everyone from the district dashboard.
What happens to my data if I cancel? ▼
Your teachers and students keep their accounts and personal apps. District-pushed apps remain unless manually removed. The district admin dashboard just becomes inaccessible.
Can I get a custom quote for a large district? ▼
Absolutely. Email us and we will work something out based on your district size and needs.
Where does my tip go? ▼
Directly to the developer. It covers hosting costs, development time, and helps justify spending more hours building new features instead of stopping.
